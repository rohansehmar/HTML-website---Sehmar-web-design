Clare - web designer

Clare graduated from University of South Wales with a 1st in Graphic Communication, and it’s easy to see why! Clare works closely with clients, using her excellent communication and problem-solving skills to help bring their visions to life. Dedicated, eager and friendly, Clare loves that every client requires something different, and she thrives from learning something new with every project!

Chris - Web developer

Chris started off by doing an Aircraft Engineering course at the University of South Wales, followed by a 6-month trip to China to teach English, before he began his career as a developer 18 months ago. Chris enjoys using his creativity to turn static designs into dynamic website layouts, ensuring a great user experience through animation and interactivity.

Ben - Tech support

Ben has been working in the IT industry for the last three years, while supporting a variety of technology systems. As an IT support assistant, Ben’s role is to provide support to all our clients, helping to find solutions to all their queries. Using his skills and ability to think fast under pressure, Ben has all the essential experience to provide the perfect solutions for our clients.
