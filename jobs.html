Are you a developer?

Our customers come from a wide range of industry sectors, and the applications we develop are as diverse as they are. We strongly believe in developing our employees' skills, and you will gain valuable experience, knowledge and expertise in:

  • Ruby on Rails
  • Postgres/MySQL databases
  • Javascript
  • HTML/CSS
  • Spree e-commerce

The development job package includes:

  • Salary between £24K - £35K depending upon experience
  • 30 days holiday (increasing to 38 with service) to include the 8 bank holidays per year
  • Profit related bonus
  • Pension scheme
  • Formal and on-the-job training
  • Cycle to Work scheme

If you would like to be part of our talented team, and help us provide innovative applications to a wide range of businesses, please contact us at jobs@sehmarwebdesigns.co.uk.
